## Benefits

I don't actually offer rewards in exchange for your support because all code and content is free for everyone, no need to subscribe or pay for anything. People who support the project, however, have higher priority and access to me when they need help with anything regarding my projects. Even so, donating doesn't guarantee it. Donate from the bottom of your heart and for the common good. Remember: all I make is free-of-charge and for everyone.

In practice, supporting us with a recurring donation gives you access to **exclusive Discord channels** and **voting rights** on specific polls related to our apps/game projects.

I repeat: everyone has access to me. It is just that those who donate have priority when support is needed, and even so donating is not a guarantee of that (although I'll do my best to help you). This priority increases according to the donated amount.

Recurring donation options other than Patreon are likely to apply a smaller fee in comparison. If you opt for a recurring donation other than Patreon, talk to me on discord so I can set up your discord benefit.

GitHub sponsors and Ko-fi also offer one-time donations.
